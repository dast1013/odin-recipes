Savory Lemon Garlic Chicken

This is a recipe my wife based on some long lost pinterest recipe. I have been working to cook more consistently for my wife's sanity so this is their take that I have learned.

It seems like magic whenever I see my wife make a sauce from the font of a pan, and it definitely felt that way when I finally understood how to make that process happen.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs or chicken breast
  • Lemons
  • Garlic Cloves or Jarred Garlic
  • Flour
  • Seasoning: Salt and Pepper, Lemon Pepper, Onion Powder

Recipe Steps

  1. Use a paper towel pat your chicken dry.
  2. Generously season your chicken with seasonings.
