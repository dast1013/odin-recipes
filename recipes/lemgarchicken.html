Savory Lemon Garlic Chicken

This is a recipe my wife based on some long lost pinterest recipe. I have been working to cook more consistently for my wife's sanity so this is their take that I have learned.

It seems like magic whenever I see my wife make a sauce from the fond of a pan, and it definitely felt that way when I finally understood how to make that process happen.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs or chicken breast
  • Lemons
  • Garlic Cloves or Jarred Garlic
  • Flour
  • Seasoning: Salt and Pepper, Lemon Pepper, Onion Powder
  • 8 tbsp of Butter
  • 1 cup of chicken broth made from bouillon powder.
  • Just of 1 1/2 lemons. Pan sear remaining lemon half
  • Soy Sauce

Recipe Steps

  1. Begin preheating frying pan with olive oil.
  2. Use a paper towel pat your chicken dry.
  3. Generously season your chicken.
  4. Sear thighs, allowing them to brown on one side before flipping to build fond.
  5. Can sear lemon half while chicken is cooking.
    6. >
  6. At this time, put pasta of choice into boiling water.
  7. Plate chicken and lemon when thoroughly cooked to rest.
  8. Add garlic to pan and cook until fragrant.
  9. Add butter and sauce mix to the pan. Reduce until thickened.
  10. Take off the heat, stir in reserved two tablespoons of butter.
  11. Combine chicken with lemon garlic sauce and serve.
